About me

Nadia De Blank is a junior freelance concept artist and the person behind ‘Faeyge’, a portmanteau meaning ‘accursed fey’. This described a character in a fantasy story that eventually inspired her to get into concept art and writing. She thrives to create and visualize the worlds that only exists in one’s imagination and turn them into reality. Every artwork she makes is required to tell a story on its own. Nadia’s passion lies with fantasy environments and characters where everything feels strangely distant yet familiar. She’s currently working on concept art that’s accompanied with a novel, which you can follow under the tag 'The 27th Marked One’. However she is available for small commissions.
